# Repurposing Plan: Secret AI Dashboard for Local Businesses

## Summary

We'll create a reel demonstrating a 'secret' AI automation platform for local service businesses. The content will focus on how this platform simplifies complex AI tasks, making automation accessible for lead generation and appointment setting, emphasizing our brand's 'we handle it for you' approach to business and AI automation.

## Content Tasks

### 1. Adapted Script: Secret AI App for Local Businesses [NEEDS HUMAN]
**Priority:** high | **Hours:** 1.5h | **Tools:** 
**Why human needed:** Needs filming, screen recording, and content approval.

Rewrite the reel's hook, body, and CTA for our brand, focusing on practical AI automation for local service companies. The original used a Curiosity Gap, which we'll maintain and amplify with a FOMO element relevant to business owners.

**Deliverables:**
- **Hook (0-3s):** Most local businesses are LOSING THOUSANDS by ignoring this secret AI automation tool.
- **Body Structure (3-45s):** Story / Demonstration. Transition from the hook to a screen recording. 'It's not some complicated code. It’s a clean dashboard that lets you…' (show simplified process: manage lead generation agents, automate follow-ups, book appointments). 'Imagine AI setting your appointments while you focus on your craft. No complex setups, no headaches.' Show a 'before' (manual, messy) vs. 'after' (clean, automated dashboard) through quick cuts or text overlays.
- **CTA (45-60s):** Stop leaving money on the table. DM 'AI LEAD' to get this done FOR YOU.
- **Psychological Trigger:** Amplified Curiosity Gap + Fear of Missing Out (FOMO). We're directly addressing a pain point (losing money) and offering a secret solution, creating a strong desire for more information.
